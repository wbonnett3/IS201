All About Will

Will Bonnett is a 2022 Highschool graduate from Parkway West High School. Recently, he has become an NCAA collegiate athlete at Brigham Young University. Find his Swimming Profile here. Will's current plan is to swim his freshman year, and serve a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints for the two upcoming years.

Highschool Swimming

Will Attended Parkway West Highschool. While there, he collected numerous awards including seven time event State Champion, Breaking 8 School Records, Class 1 Record Holder in the 100 Breastroke, and 2021 MSHSAA Class 1 Swimmer of the Year

Club Swimming

Will swam for Parkway Swim Club. In his 13 years with the club, he broke 8 club records in the 17-18 age group and Open age group, set two Ozark Records, and was Parkway Swimmer of the Year in 2022.

BYU Career

In his freshman year, Will has already acheived the 2nd fastest time in school history for the 200 Breastroke, and has high hopes of attending the NCAA Championships in March of 2023.
